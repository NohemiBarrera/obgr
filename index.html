About

Experienced sound engineer, sound designer and sound editor professional with over 10 years of international touring experience. My credentials include leading live engineer for FOH & Monitors for a wide range of artists including Latin Grammy winners, Mon Laferte and Carla Morrison. In addition to audio, I've lead tours in roles such as a Production Manager, Tour Manager, and Stage Manager.

I'm passionate about my work and sharing my experience with others. When I'm not on tour, I teach sound engineering and sound editing at REC MUSICA Centro de Estudios Musicales.
